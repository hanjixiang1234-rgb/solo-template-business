## Data Review Template

### Daily review

Track for each post:
- publish time
- impressions
- likes
- saves
- comments
- follows
- profile visits

### Interpretation rules

High impressions, low saves:
- hook may work, content body is weak

Low impressions, high saves:
- topic is useful, cover or title may be too weak

High saves and comments:
- good candidate for series expansion

Low across all metrics:
- rewrite hook or cover and do not repeat the exact angle

### Weekly review questions

1. Which topic generated the highest saves?
2. Which title format got the best first response?
3. Which posts made people comment about their own symptoms or routines?
4. Which topics can connect to a future checklist or guide?

### Week-1 benchmark focus

- save rate
- comment quality
- profile visits

Do not overfocus on:
- raw like count alone
