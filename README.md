## Solo Media Business

This project is a one-person creator business.

The chosen model:
- Build a faceless Xiaohongshu account for a China-based audience.
- Publish practical wellness and light养生 image-text content.
- Use content as the front-end and products, private-domain conversion, and later recommendations as the back-end offer.

Why this model:
- Better fit for a China-based operator than relying on overseas marketplaces first.
- Can be run without face, team, or inventory.
- The topic has broad demand and strong save/share behavior on Xiaohongshu.
- Allows low-cost testing through content before product expansion.

Business rules:
- One main platform only in phase 1.
- No live streaming requirement.
- No disease-treatment promises.
- No miracle-cure language.
- No generic pseudo-science content.
- Every post must be understandable, low-risk, and easy to save.

Revenue targets:
- Day 30: 20 published notes, one clear account positioning, and the first lead magnet or low-ticket offer.
- Day 60: 40 to 60 published notes and the first validated digital product offer.
- Day 90: stable weekly content, one repeatable conversion path, and meaningful first revenue.

Role split:
- I handle strategy, content system design, topic selection, scripts, offer structure, and operating docs.
- You only provide account access, verification, payout setup, and occasional directional feedback.

Current phase:
- Phase 1: health-account positioning, content system, and first batch publishing.
