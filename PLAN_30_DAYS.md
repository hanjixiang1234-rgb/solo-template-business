## 30-Day Execution Plan

### Objective

Launch a faceless Xiaohongshu account with a clear niche, 20 published notes, and an early conversion path to a lead magnet or low-ticket digital product.

### Days 1-3: Positioning

Tasks:
- Lock the platform: Xiaohongshu only.
- Lock the audience: freelancers, solo operators, and small service sellers in China.
- Lock the account theme: AI productivity plus one-person business systems.
- Define account name, bio direction, visual style, and content pillars.
- Prepare the account and verification checklist.

Success criteria:
- One clear audience.
- One clear promise.
- One clear content style.

### Days 4-10: Content System

Tasks:
- Build the first 15 to 20 topic ideas.
- Draft the first 10 publishable notes.
- Define a reusable note structure and hook library.
- Create simple cover rules and caption rules.
- Set up a document structure for scripts and iteration tracking.

Success criteria:
- The first publishing batch is ready.
- Content production can repeat without starting from zero each day.

### Days 11-17: First Publishing Sprint

Tasks:
- Publish the first 7 to 10 notes.
- Track saves, comments, follows, and profile visits.
- Identify which hooks and topics get the best response.
- Refine account bio and pinned-note direction if needed.

Success criteria:
- Real audience response data exists.
- The account positioning is clearer after the first posts.

### Days 18-24: Offer Connection

Tasks:
- Build a free checklist or mini template as a lead magnet.
- Draft the first low-ticket template pack connected to the best-performing topic.
- Add soft call-to-action language into selected notes.
- Document frequent questions and objections from early engagement.

Success criteria:
- The account is no longer content-only.
- One backend offer exists in draft form.

### Days 25-30: Optimization

Tasks:
- Improve titles, first-screen hooks, and cover style.
- Rewrite the weakest notes using the best-performing patterns.
- Prepare the second content batch.
- Decide whether to add a second platform later based on output capacity.

Success criteria:
- One optimization pass is complete.
- A repeatable posting system exists.

### Operating cadence

Daily:
- 1 new note draft
- 1 published note or scheduled note
- 1 iteration task based on account feedback

Weekly:
- Review top-performing topics
- Keep, kill, or rewrite weak formats
- Push one idea toward a monetizable asset

### Hard constraints

- No second platform in the first 30 days.
- No broad lifestyle content.
- No dependency on video production, voice recording, or live streaming.
