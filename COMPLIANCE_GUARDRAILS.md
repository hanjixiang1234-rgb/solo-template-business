## Health Content Guardrails

This project touches health-adjacent content.
That means all content must stay inside lifestyle and general wellness boundaries.

### Allowed direction

- daily routine suggestions
- sleep hygiene habits
- hydration reminders
- movement and rest prompts
- gentle food and schedule ideas
- seasonal lifestyle adjustments

### Avoid

- diagnosis
- treatment plans
- cure language
- drug recommendations
- supplement claims with certainty
- statements like "must", "guaranteed", or "cures"
- disease-specific advice without authoritative medical sourcing

### Content style rule

Each note should be:
- simple
- low-risk
- easy to try
- framed as daily habit sharing

### Safety footer rule

For sensitive posts, include a soft note:
- this is daily lifestyle sharing
- it does not replace professional medical advice

### Monetization rule

Do not connect content to health-product sales too early.
Build trust first through save-worthy practical content.
