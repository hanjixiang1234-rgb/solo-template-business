## Cover Click System

Built on the reference-account review completed on March 16, 2026.

### The three cover lanes we should use

1. Minimal function card
- Light background
- one black headline block
- one simple icon or body-part illustration
- best for: brain fog, cold hands, tired mornings, eye strain

2. Checklist save card
- Beige or off-white background
- dense but structured text
- yellow highlight lines
- best for: routines, avoid/do lists, meal rhythm, late-night recovery

3. Persona meme card
- Cartoon, meme, sticker, or emotionally expressive figure
- stronger attitude in the title
- best for: embarrassment, self-care, women-specific discomfort, office-worker survival topics

### Headline formulas to repeat

1. `别再___了，你的___需要被___`
- Example:
- `别再硬扛了，你的情绪需要被看见`

2. `___的人，真的要试试___`
- Example:
- `久坐的人，真的要试试中午站5分钟`

3. `把___一点点养回来`
- Example:
- `把清晰的脑子一点点养回来`

4. `___一旦___，身体就会___`
- Example:
- `臀部一旦发热，身体就好了大半`

5. `原来___也能靠___`
- Example:
- `原来下午发沉也能靠这3步缓回来`

6. `___小方法`
- Example:
- `冬天快速起床的小方法`

### What makes people click

1. One very specific discomfort
- `脑雾`
- `起床累`
- `手脚冰`
- `眼浊`
- `午后发沉`

2. One surprising method
- `吹风机`
- `迈大步子`
- `听儿歌`
- `热敷`
- `饭后站一会`

3. One soft promise
- `缓一点`
- `稳一点`
- `清晰一点`
- `舒服一点`
- `养回来一点`

### What our next batch should look like

Preferred visual direction:
- warm off-white
- pale pink
- soft beige
- dark gray headline
- one accent color only

Preferred tone:
- gentle but not weak
- specific but not medical
- useful enough to save
- emotionally accurate

### Production rule

Before making a cover, each post must answer:

1. What exact discomfort is this about?
2. Who is this for?
3. What single method or shift is being promised?
4. Can the viewer understand it in one glance?
5. Would someone want to save it instead of only like it?
