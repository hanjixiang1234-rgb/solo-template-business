### Profile 1: `百岁老人培养计划`

Observed strengths:
- Cover is extremely simple.
- A single icon or cartoon sits in the center.
- The text is huge, dark, and readable from far away.
- The background is usually white or light pink, which makes the text look calm and useful.

What the best-performing covers are doing:
- One symptom, one method, one promise.
- The topic is concrete instead of broad.
- The feeling is “this might fix a specific discomfort I already have.”

High-signal examples from the profile:
- `一把吹风机，吹走这么多的寒`
- `告别脑雾，把清晰的脑子一点点养回来`
- `亲测有效！养回星星眼，去眼浊就靠它了！`
- `别再硬扛了，你的情绪需要被看见`
- `社死预警⚠️身上居然有这么多的“隐性臭味”`

Why it works:
- Uses body-state words like `寒` `脑雾` `眼浊` `情绪` `臭味`.
- Gives the viewer a very fast “this is about me” moment.
- The drawing style looks non-threatening, so the content feels light and save-worthy.

### Profile 2: `懒系长生诀`

Observed strengths:
- This account is much more “save-first” than “emotion-first.”
- Covers look like mini cheat sheets, formulas, lists, and highlighted notes.
- It uses a consistent panda-like visual system and beige tone.
- The eye is pulled by yellow highlight bars and dense information blocks.

High-signal examples from the profile:
- `养回清晰大脑的实用方法`
- `身体炎症全部消失的tips`
- `必须要戒掉的这些隐形支出`
- `万能去污公式，全屋清洁小技巧`

Why it works:
- The account sells “organized useful information,” not just wellness.
- People click because they expect a full list, not because they want inspiration.
- The format makes users feel “I should save this before I forget.”

Important takeaway:
- Not every wellness account wins with warm emotion.
- Some win because they make themselves look like a practical database.

### Profile 3: `铁血老太培养计划`

Observed strengths:
- Strong persona before strong professionalism.
- The account feels like a sharp, funny, slightly rebellious friend.
- Covers often use meme-like faces, cartoons, screenshots, or exaggerated character visuals.
- The image style is less “clean wellness” and more “identity + attitude.”

High-signal examples from the profile:
- `女孩子真的要试试迈大步子走路！`
- `发现了获得勇气的办法：听儿歌`
- `女孩不必因腋下出汗而感到尴尬`
- `冬天快速起床的13个邪修小方法`
- `一些冬天减少生病提高生活幸福感的小tips`

Why it works:
- It directly addresses identity groups such as `女孩子` and `脆皮人`.
- It uses unexpected methods like `听儿歌` and `迈大步子走路`.
- The audience clicks out of curiosity plus self-recognition.
- The tone feels alive, not textbook-like.

Important takeaway:
- Wellness content does not need to sound “correct.”
- It needs to sound like it understands the viewer’s real embarrassment, laziness, fear, or small suffering.

### Cross-category visual reference: `塔塔的时尚笔记本`

This is not a wellness account, but the cover system is worth borrowing.

Observed strengths:
- Real-person photo as the main visual anchor.
- Big title strips placed directly on the image.
- Strong repeated headline packaging.
- The same hook is reused many times across different posts.

High-signal examples from the profile:
- `快醒醒，你缺的不是衣服，是穿搭思路啊！！`
- `超详细自拍教程，一个人就能拍出爱豆同款`

Why it matters for us:
- It proves repeated headline packaging works.
- Once a click-worthy sentence is found, the same sentence can become a series.

### Shared click-driving logic across the sample set

1. They all lead with a specific discomfort or identity.
- `脑雾`
- `眼浊`
- `腋下出汗`
- `起床困难`
- `脆皮人`
- `女孩子`

2. They make the viewer curious with one unexpected object or action.
- `吹风机`
- `迈大步子走路`
- `听儿歌`
- `13个邪修小方法`

3. They are written for fast emotional recognition.
- The user sees the cover and thinks `这是我`.

4. They keep the promise small and believable.
- Not “彻底治好”.
- More like `一点点养回来` `减少生病` `提高幸福感` `把脑子养清晰`.

5. They optimize for save, not only for like.
- Lists
- formulas
- tips
- avoid/do
- one-screen takeaway

### Shared image-making rules

1. One-screen clarity
- One main hook only.
- Do not stack too many ideas on the cover.

2. A single focal point
- One icon
- one object
- one face
- or one meme figure

3. High text contrast
- Dark text on light background works best in the sampled wellness pages.

4. Lots of whitespace
- The successful covers do not feel crowded.
- Even text-heavy ones still keep one dominant block.

5. Stable visual system
- Repeated mascot
- repeated background tone
- repeated title strip style

### What we should borrow for `轻养生笔记`

From `百岁老人培养计划`:
- Minimal pastel cover
- one symptom + one method hook
- soft but direct body-state language

From `懒系长生诀`:
- checklist and formula-style save posts
- highlighted key lines
- “worth keeping” information packaging

From `铁血老太培养计划`:
- stronger persona
- more human tone
- more identity-based hooks

From `塔塔的时尚笔记本`:
- once a headline wins, repeat the package as a series
- use one recognizable cover system so users know it is us at first glance

### What we should avoid

- Vague topics like `春天怎么养生`
- Covers with no specific body part, symptom, or user type
- Heavy textbook or fake doctor wording
- Medical certainty language
- Too many decorative elements
- Overly neat corporate-looking layouts

### Direct conclusion

The best-performing wellness covers are not trying to look “professional.”

They are trying to look:
- relevant
- specific
- emotionally accurate
- instantly understandable
- easy to save

For our account, the winning path is:
- `轻症状`
- `轻方法`
- `轻压力`
- `强钩子`
- `强封面识别`
