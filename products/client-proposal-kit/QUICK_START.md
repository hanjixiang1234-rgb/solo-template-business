## Client Proposal Kit

This kit helps a freelancer or small service business move a prospect from inquiry to signed scope faster.

Included files:
- `PROPOSAL_TEMPLATE.md`
- `QUOTE_TEMPLATE.md`
- `SCOPE_TEMPLATE.md`
- `FOLLOW_UP_EMAILS.md`

Recommended use order:
1. Duplicate all files.
2. Customize business name, service, and pricing language.
3. Send `QUOTE_TEMPLATE.md` for simple deals.
4. Send `PROPOSAL_TEMPLATE.md` for larger or more strategic deals.
5. Use `SCOPE_TEMPLATE.md` before final approval.
6. Use `FOLLOW_UP_EMAILS.md` if the client goes quiet.

Positioning note:
- Keep the proposal outcome-focused.
- Keep the quote simple.
- Keep the scope exact.
- Keep follow-up messages short and useful.
