## Media Strategy

### Chosen platform

Phase 1 platform:
- Xiaohongshu

Reason:
- Best fit for a faceless, text-forward, one-person workflow.
- Lower production burden than starting with video-heavy channels.
- Good match for practical, save-worthy, wellness-oriented content.

Official creator platform references verified on March 15, 2026:
- Xiaohongshu creator platform: https://creator.xiaohongshu.com/creator/home
- Bilibili creator center: https://member.bilibili.com/platform/home
- Douyin creator center: https://creator.douyin.com/creator-micro/home

### Account concept

Working account name:
- Gentle Wellness Notes

Chinese direction:
- `轻养生笔记`

Core promise:
- Share simple daily wellness habits that ordinary people can understand and try without pressure.

### Audience

Primary audience:
- women and men interested in daily wellness
- office workers with irregular schedules
- people who like easy-to-save health routines
- users who prefer gentle lifestyle content over heavy medical explanations

### Content pillars

1. Daily gentle wellness
- sleep, hydration, movement, routine, seasonal habits

2. Office-worker recovery habits
- long sitting, eye strain, late nights, meal rhythm, low-energy afternoons

3. Easy food and drink ideas
- ingredient combinations, warm drinks, simple meal reminders

4. Traditional-style wellness framing
- body warmth, daily rhythm, seasonal adjustment, gentle self-care

### Content format

Primary format:
- image-plus-text note

Post structure:
- sharp cover hook
- problem setup
- simplified framework
- copyable steps
- soft CTA

### Monetization path

Stage 1:
- grow targeted attention

Stage 2:
- offer a free checklist or mini guide

Stage 3:
- sell low-ticket wellness guides or meal/routine checklists

Stage 4:
- bundle the highest-demand assets or connect to compliant product recommendations

### Positioning constraints

- No fake doctor identity
- No diagnosis or treatment advice
- No drug recommendations
- No reliance on personal exposure
