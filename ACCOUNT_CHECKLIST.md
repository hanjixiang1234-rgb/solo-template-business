## Account and Identity Checklist

### What you need to provide

Required:
- A dedicated email for account operations
- A Xiaohongshu account or a fresh account to configure
- Phone verification when the platform requires it
- Real-name verification if the platform or later monetization path requires it
- A payout route for later product sales

Later, if we expand:
- A second platform account such as Bilibili
- A simple payment or delivery account for digital products

### What I can do without you

- Choose the niche and account direction
- Build the content system
- Write note scripts and titles
- Design the offer ladder
- Prepare publishing and iteration documents
- Define monetization timing and product structure

### What I cannot do on your behalf

- Complete identity verification using your legal identity
- Receive platform SMS codes without shared access
- Link payment accounts that require your confirmation
- Approve platform agreements on your behalf if they require direct legal acceptance

### Minimal unlock sequence

Step 1:
- Open or provide access to the account email

Step 2:
- Open or provide access to the Xiaohongshu account

Step 3:
- Complete phone or identity verification if requested by the platform

Step 4:
- Hand over the working account environment so publishing can begin

### Decision rule

If a platform creates avoidable friction in phase 1, we do not force it.
We keep the business model and simplify the delivery path.
