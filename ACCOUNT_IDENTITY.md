## Account Identity

### Recommended account name

Primary:
- `轻养生笔记`

Alternatives:
- `打工人养一养`
- `慢慢养回来`
- `今天也轻养一下`

### Bio options

Option 1:
- 分享普通人能做的轻养生习惯
- 不贩卖焦虑，不讲复杂大道理
- 记录那些真的能坚持的小事

Option 2:
- 给打工人的轻养生笔记
- 讲作息、饮食、节奏和日常状态
- 只做容易收藏的简单内容

### Pinned-note direction

Pinned note 1:
- 这个账号会更新什么

Pinned note 2:
- 打工人最容易忽略的 5 个轻养生习惯

Pinned note 3:
- 新来的先看这一篇：轻养生从哪里开始

### Visual direction

Cover style:
- large dark text
- warm light background
- one clear hook only
- no cluttered decoration

Recommended colors:
- off-white
- warm beige
- muted brown
- dark gray text

### Tone

- gentle
- grounded
- save-worthy
- not preachy
- not overly technical

### Comment strategy

- encourage one-word responses
- invite people to save the post
- ask about common daily struggles

### Phase-1 monetization stance

- grow trust first
- do not sell health products too early
- later connect to checklists, routine guides, and simple paid digital assets
